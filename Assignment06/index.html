Hearty Chicken Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Cornish game hen, whole
  • 2 sweet onions, chopped
  • 1 garlic bulb, chopped
  • Carrots, to taste
  • Celery, to taste
  • Asparagus, one "bunch" should do!
  • White mushrooms, sliced (optional)
  • Celery leaves (from your celery), chopped
  • Rubbed sage (Just a pinch!)
  • Bay leaves (2 or 3)
  • Dried parlsey
  • Two 10.5 oz. cans beef consomme
  • One 10.5 oz. can beef broth
  • One small carton Swanson beef gravy

Directions

  1. Place whole cornish hen into stock pot with chopped onions and garlic.
  2. Add sage, parsley, and bay leaves
  3. Add beef consomme, beef broth, and gravy
  4. Cover, bring to a low simmer and let cook until cornish hen is cooked through
  5. Remove cornish hen from pot and strip meat from bones, add meat back to broth
  6. (If you want mushrooms, you can add them now!)
  7. Add carrots and celery, cook until softened
  8. Add asparagus, cook until ready
  9. Ta-da! Soup's on!
